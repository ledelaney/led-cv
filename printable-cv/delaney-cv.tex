\section{\fontsize{16}{48}\selectfont Ongoing Teaching Projects}

\begin{itemize}

\item{\textbf{A digital community approach to large-enrollment biology lecture} $\vert$ Outlines the development and use of an instructional ``livestream model'' that leverages popular social media apps like YouTube and Discord to faciliate high-quality online education and community building at scale.}

\item{\textbf{Examining the efficacy of two-stage exam models} $\vert$ Studies the effect of two-stage exams on longer-term content retention, with an emphasis on variations in group composition.\textsuperscript{1}}

\item{\textbf{Effect of course design and structure on student sense of belonging} $\vert$ Uses pre-post surveys over multiple quarters to assess various affective metrics with an emphasis on student sense of belonging.\textsuperscript{1}}

\item{\textbf{Accessibility and inclusion in the digital classroom} $\vert$ Focuses on the effects of digital instruction on students with disability accommodations.\textsuperscript{1,2}}
